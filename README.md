# SicoTech-Mobile-Phone-Analysis
SicoTech Mobile Phone Sales Analysis is an Excel-based dashboard project that visualizes sales performance, brand rankings, storage preferences, and color trends. It highlights top and underperforming brands, total revenue, sales volume, and discount impact, providing actionable insights to optimize inventory, pricing, and product strategy.

**Sales Performance & Product Optimization**

**Business Problem / Objective**

SicoTech management wants to understand overall mobile phone sales performance and identify:

- Which brands drive the highest revenue

- Which brands are underperforming

- What storage capacities customers prefer

- What colors sell the most

- The distribution between high and low sales segments

- How pricing impacts revenue

**The key business question:**

How can SicoTech optimize product mix, pricing, and brand focus to maximize revenue and improve inventory strategy?
